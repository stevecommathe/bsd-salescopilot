## Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

**Q: What is your MOQ?**

Generally, MOQ is 1 x 40ft FCL (Full Container Load) per brand or category.

However, we may be able to mix certain brands together if they load from the same or similar origin location. Customer should confirm brands of interest and potential volume per brand, and we'll advise on mixing options.

---

## Shipping Terms

**Q: What shipping terms do you offer?**

We generally operate on CIF terms (Cost, Insurance, Freight — delivered to destination port).

For some products, we may be able to work on EXW or FOB terms — discuss with account manager.

**Q: Do you offer DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)?**

No, we don't offer DDP terms. We're an international B2B distributor and work on CIF terms (to destination port).

We don't import, clear, or distribute locally in any market.

Exception: For US-origin products shipping within the USA, we can potentially arrange delivery to warehouse.
