### Shipping & Delivery

**Q: What are your shipping terms?**

Generally, we operate on CIF shipping terms (Cost, Insurance, Freight — delivered to your destination port). For some products we may be able to work on EXW or FOB terms, to be discussed with your dedicated account manager.

**Q: Can you work on DDP shipping terms?**

We do not offer DDP unfortunately. We do not import, clear, or distribute locally in any market — we are an international B2B distributor, generally working on CIF terms (to your chosen destination port).

Exception: If you are in the USA and the products are US-origin, then we can potentially do DDP.

**Q: Can you deliver to my warehouse?**

No, we do not import. Our incoterms are CIF (to destination port).

Exception: If you are based in the USA and the products are in the USA, then we can potentially deliver to your warehouse.

**Q: Can you deliver pallets to my consolidator in USA or X market?**

It depends on the brand and the volume per brand. Please confirm both and your dedicated account manager will advise.

**Q: Do you ship to [any destination]?**

We can ship to any destination port worldwide.

**Q: What is the transit time?**

This depends on origin location and destination location. Please confirm your brand/product range of interest and destination port, and we will confirm the transit time.

**Q: Give me CIF price for my port**

Please confirm your destination port and brand of interest. We will then provide a quote for you.

**Q: What is the origin port for that product?**

It may vary. Your account manager will advise separately once you confirm your brands of interest.

### Lead Time & Shelf Life

**Q: What is the lead time?**

Lead time from order deposit receipt to ready-to-load is roughly 2-3 weeks. However, it varies per brand, product, and category. All lead time details are shown on the portal.

We do not delay — once a deposit is received, we work as quickly as we can to load.

**Q: What is the shelf life?**

Shelf life on manufacture and shelf life expected on loading are shown on the portal for each category of product. We understand the importance of shelf life and only load according to expectations stated on the portal.
